Looking Ahead

The ‘Quest for Knowledge’ is undoubtedly a constant search for everyone, and it has been a Universal Truth so far, which will also continue to be the same for eternity.

To contribute towards this Quest, in a phased manner FRANCIS Publications will be engaged in:

          Publication of Books
          Publication of Skill Enhancing Materials for Students & Professionals
          Conducting of Customized Training / Mentoring Programs for Students, Institutions & Organizations

The Idea Incubation is also at work earnestly and with the passage of time, many more activities will be added on as appropriate to the Need based on the concept of Personal and Social Development towards Social Change.
