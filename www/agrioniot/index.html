We make agriculture Smarter Automatable Profitable

What we want to do?

we want to develop a System to monitor a forming land, quality of the sail, feed the required amount of water and monitor the electricity usage of the water feeding. The Solution college all the data in the forming land and send the information to the cloud. The cloud system analysis the data from varies sources and give the right feedback to the former and Government such that what kind of cultivation suitable for this land, which seeds make more profitable for this weather condition. It provides a statical information of total amount of Water user for Forming land and meter value of Electricity used in the forming land to the Government.

Market Problem

In India 70% of people are working in Agriculture field. The are so many problem for the farmer to manage the farming and make money out of it. We believe the smart technology enable them to make better profit and manage the farming land.
