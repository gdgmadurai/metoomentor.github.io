Milestones

1949 - College started with courses in UG Economics, History and Mathematics

1953 - Introduction of B.A. Tamil

1957 - Introduction of B.A. English, B.Sc. Physics, Chemistry and Zoology

1958 - Introduction of M.A. Tamil

1959 - Introduction of M.A. Economics, M.Sc. Maths and M.Sc. Zoology

1961 - Introduction of M.A. English

1965 - Introduction of B.Sc. Botany

1968 - Introduction of B.A. Economics in Tamil

1970 - Introduction of B.B.A.

1972 - Introduction of semester pattern in Post Graduate courses

1976 - Introduction of semester pattern in Under Graduate courses

1979 - Introduction of M.Phil. in Tamil and Zoology and M.Sc. in Botany. Department of Tamil and Zoology were upgraded as research centres

1980 - Introduction of M.Sc. Physics

1983 - Introduction of M.Phil. in English

1984 - Department of Botany was upgraded as a research centre

1985 - Introduction of M.Phil. Botany and B.Com.

1986 - Introduction of M.Sc. Chemistry

1987 - Introduction of B.Sc. Computer Science.College became autonomous

1994 - Change of nomenclature from B.Sc. Zoology to Industrial Micro Biology

1998 - Co-education was introduced at the UG level.Introduction of M.Sc Microbiology (Self-financed)

1999 - Introduction of M.Sc. Computer Science (self-financed) and M.M.S.(self-financed)

2000 - Introduction of M.F.C.(self-financed), B.Com.(self-financed) and P.G.D.C.A.(self-financed)

2001 - Introduction of B.Sc.(Biotech), P.G.D.C.I.T. & College received the Five Star status from NAAC

2002 - Introduction of B.C.A.(self-financed) and Certificate Course in Biotechnology

2003 - Introduction of CBCS - Choice Based Credit System

2004 - Department of Chemistry was upgraded as a research centre

2005 - Department of English was upgraded as a research centre, Introduction of Certificate, Diploma and Advanced Diploma courses in Tamil, Economics and Business Administration

2006 - Introduction of P.G.D.M.I.D.D.. Department of Economics was upgraded as a research centre

2007 - College Re-Accredited with "A" Grade by NAAC. Introduction of M.Sc. Bio-Resource Management (self-financed)

2008 - Introduction of B.Sc. Computer Science (self-financed), B.Sc. Information Technology (self-financed), M.Phil. Physics (self-financed) and M.Phil. Chemistry (self-financed)

2009 - Introduction of M.Phil.(self-financed) Commerce

2010 - Introduction of M.H.R.D. (self-financed) and M.Phil. (self-financed) Mathematics. Introduction of Women's Studies Centre (Under UGC Grant)

2011 - Introduction of B.B.A. (self-financed)

2012 - Introduction of M.Phil.(self-financed) Economics, B.A.(self-financed) Economics (English Medium) and B.A.(self-financed) English. Department of Mathematics was upgraded as a research centre.
