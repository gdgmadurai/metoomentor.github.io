Tie Ups

MEDICAL TRANSPORTATION

    Our Non-Emergency Medical Transportation Service focuses on providing transportation from home to destination and back without you having to battle traffic jams or face any kind of stress.

    We also provide an accompanying nurse to escort the patient for checkups, treatment & therapies from home.

HOUSE CALL DOCTORS

    Have The Doctor Come to You. Our LCare Doctors service caters for patients whom are unable to endure the journey to their medical centers. House Call Doctor Service covers basic procedures & treatments. This however is NOT an emergency service.

HOME NURSING CARE

    Have a registered nurse care for your loved ones at home. Our Home Nursing Services caters for your specific needs and can be booked by the hour or long term. With the rise in nursing home costs and the lack in professional care in most homes, Our home nursing service is fast becoming the best caring option with no worries.

HOME PHYSIOTHERAPY

    Have your regular physiotherapy sessions at the comfort your own home by qualified & experienced physiotherapists. No more long waits at the hospital. Start your road to recovery at home.

OUT PATIENT LIFE CARE

    Outpatient Life Care provides medical care or treatment that does not require an overnight stay in a hospital or medical facility. Such treatments can be administered in a clinic or even at home. Common ailments such as fever, cough, mild vomiting or diarrhea and many more can be treated without much trouble without having to make a trip to the hospital.

Customer Care

    Our LCare Doctors are ready to help our patients by providing access to telephone medical advice 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. All you have to do is call our doctor's office/Support Team. After regular office hours, follow the prompts to connect with our team of LCare medical staff who answer calls and provide medical advice and information over phone.

    If necessary, our staff can consult an on-call LCare physician, advise you according to the emergency, or schedule you treatment in case urgent care appointment required at one of the practices with Urgent Care.

    Please note: if you are experiencing a life-threatening emergency, call to our support team or local emergency number immediately so we can follow up on your care
