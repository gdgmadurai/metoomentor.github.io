Metoomentor GDG Madurai Events Report






HTML5 and Web App Development.

The Overall summary about the event is spreading the word about Google developer Group, Google Student Ambassador program and Hands On training on HTML5, Web App development using Intel XDK. The end of the day we conducted a small hackathon where student able to design a Android application using Intel XDK

GDG for Women Orientation

The Overall summary about the event is spreading the word about GDG for Women, Motivating women in Madurai and exposing them to the research and Technological field. The event includes a half days session on brainstorming and developing Android application for their idea.

Android Workshop

The Overall summary about the event is setting up the Android Development environment and start writing their First android app using Eclipse. A small session on how to use Social media to market your android application. creating a developer network to do beta testing of your android application.



GDG Women Python Hackathon

The Overall summary about the event is spreading the word about GDG for Women Madurai Platform, Playground where people could come in and hack the solutions, pitch new idea and make themselves a team to work and engage to increase their skills.

Google Cloud Technology Hackathon

The Overall summary about the event is making people to understand about Google Cloud platform and spreading the word about GDG Madurai Platform, Playground where people could come in and hack the solutions, pitch new idea and make themselves a team to work and engage to increase their skills.

International Women’s Day Hackathon

The Overall summary about the event is organizing the International women’s day hackathon in Madurai. This is the first time in South part of Tamil nadu more than 600 girls coming together to solve the issues of women’s in rural area. These girls use technology to develop a solution which can help women’s to solve the day to day problem in rural area. This is two day 36 hours hackathon focusing on developing mobile application for women safety and women empowerment.

Google IO Extended Madurai 2015

The Overall summary about the event is showing the live stream of the Google IO 15 from San Francisco to the developers in Madurai followed by the codelab, hackathon and startupExpo. The event was conducted in four days in four different venue to give equal oppertunity to everyone. The hackathon event extended further away from Madurai 40 KM a small village called papanayakampatti to give exposure to the rural developers. The Developers from Madurai got opportunity to get to know about the Google Latest products and new technology
