About

Platform – Bengaluru, a home for thousands of brave and passionate artists, activists and entrepreneurs. This is an effort to provide free space and encourage the spirited souls. Here is a Bengaluru ’ s crowd funded festival –PLATFORM.

Come experience the streets of Namma Bengaluru like never before. Street performances, live art, food and shopping, everything is here. All the fun from the streets and no traffic.

We will all be there with a promise to make your presence worthwhile. Make sure you stop by!

The Idea

Learn From These Great Folks

  • The story behind

    Disclaimer: All the characters in this story are not fictious. The names are changed to avoid online stalkers.

    Once upon a time, not so long ago, there was a young woman.A young woman, lets call her ‘Bag’ya, who loved to make pretty bags from recycled clothes. She made a big pile of bags, but knew not where to sell. Few places were open, but she had to pay a big buck to get any. Disappointed girl worried about how to clear her pile of bags. But her bravery and passion stayed strong. Her friendwho noticed, gave a thought to solve Bagya’s problem. Soon she realized, there were a many like Bagya, who had been in need of a Platform which was free; a platform where she gets due appreciation.

    Then came the idea of PLATFORM, a free space for those driven by passion andpurpose

  • The logo

    Designer at Cottage

    The bucolic India has a katte or a small platform under the shadow of a peepal or a banyan tree. This is the adda for all the village folks. They meet at the katte for their everyday dose of Teen Patti, to discuss issues, and occasionally for performances. We at Platform want to build a ‘katte’ for Bengalureans. A place for the unsung artists, activists and entrepreneurs to interact and showcase their work

    As a society, we talk about various issues ranging from our personal lives, politics, country and whatnot. But what about those conversations wherein we talk about our passions - our dreams to change things around us, which are not just dreams anymore. The warrior in you wants to learn something new, aspire to be better, rekindle the passion, gather courage and pursue your passion.

    Platform is the katte for Bengalureans to strike such conversations. It’s more than a place to see art. It’s a place to start a dialogue, to know the story behind each passionate soul. It is an attempt to remind us all the very fundamental of human purposes - to be there for each other.

  • Crowd funded

    Getting popular by the day, the term ‘Crowd Funded’ is not just about the funds. It is about the crowd, fuelling an Idea with their support and energy. An idea may be a brain child of anyone. But the moment the idea is thrown across the crowd, the worthy idea is picked and endorsed by the crowd.

    Here is Platform, Bengaluru’s first crowd funded festival to support the ones driven by passion.

  • Meet the covvu

    During one of our discussion sessions, our designer Srinidhi Gurunath, a true creative soul was penning all that we told pictorially with aid of the covvu. Seemed like, the covvuu was the master who made more sense of our ideas and thoughts than the others on the table. That’s when we realized, covvu would do better in passing the idea of Platform to fellow bengalureans

  • What to expect?

    Meet hundreds of souls driven by passion and courage, do not hesitate, start a conversation, know their story. It’s interesting to know what they do and why they do, what they do.

    Be ready to unleash your creative side. Vent out your anger on wrath wall, with paint and brush.

    Hand made gifts anyone?! We have clay modelling and pottery section where you can get your hands dirty.

    Treat your creative self with mouth-watering food!

    Wait, entertainment? Check the street performers.

    Get your kids, get your pets, get everyone. This shall be an experience; you do not want to miss.

This is all about ‘Serious Fun’.
